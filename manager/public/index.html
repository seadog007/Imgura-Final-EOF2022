Restart Instance 🔄

Update WAF 🛡️

每 30 秒你可以更新你的 WAF（最多 255 字元），之後 5 分鐘內（1 回合）無法獲得防禦分。注意：你的 WAF 是公開的，可以被所有隊伍看見。

You can update your WAF rule every 30 seconds. The length limit of your WAF rule is 255 characters. But you cannot get defense points in the round you update your WAF rule. Note that your WAF rule is publicly readable. All other teams can see it.
